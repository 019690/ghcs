    the 1hr self mastery session

    Ksh. 4,500

    In a 1hour Zoom session, we explore what you have in your mind as a priority that, if addressed, you will have one foot in, or two, towards your goal. You will gain clarity during the session and chart the best way to unleash your maximum potential. Suppose the session uncovers additional areas that you need further clarity on. In that case, you can book additional individual sessions or take up the 6-week Clarity and Growth program, which allows for an intensive approach.

    The 6-weeks Clarity and Growth Program

    Ksh. 18,000

    The 6-weeks program covers self-awareness, thoughts and emotions, goal identification and clarity, realistic and achievable action planning, and goal attainment. This holistic program focuses on helping you understand key success pillars that determine how you navigate life. During and after the sessions, you will apply the learnings to real-life experiences to empower your decision-making and create positive momentum toward your goals.

    The Mastering My Relationships Program

    Kshs. 12,000

    Relationships play a vital role in the quality of our lives. In the program, you will access tools, activities, and strategies that practically support navigating the relationship you want to address. This is a four weeks program. At the end of the four weeks, you will have the ability to navigate the relationship positively.

    the 3 months accountability & groth program

    Ksh. 90,000

    This intensive three-month program narrows down to one particular issue, such as building self-confidence, healthy relationships, and career decisions, among other topics. Building healthy and helpful habits requires guided repetition, and a three months program provides sufficient time to help you navigate the issues that will arise during the transformation process. The program combines online and one-on-one sessions for output and results optimization.
